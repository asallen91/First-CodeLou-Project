About the Band

Isolation Tank Ensemble is an instrumental band from Louisville, KY currently comprised of 5 members dedicated to bringing together classical instruments along side modern style music and out of the ordinary time signatures, creating a new sound they like to call "Trash Prog". The band is currently working towards writing and recording their first album and they hope to have a few demo tracks available for an EP soon.

Mateo Barnet

The fearless unoffical leader and one of the founders of the Band, Mateo plays the Electic and Acoustic Mandolin.

Nick Evans

The backbone for the beats and one of the foundersof the Band, Nick plays the Drums, and plays them as wild as it gets. He's very particular about his metal, but when it comes to the metal that Isolation Tank Ensemble brings to the world, he loves playing it.

Kirstyn Blandy

The Violinist for the band, Kirstyn is classically trained and has been playing Violin since "time?", she joined the band in Feburary 2018 when Mateo reached out to her seeking a talented violinist. She brings a very unique sound to band by playing her Acoustic Violin through an amplifier. She's gone above and beyond in proving that one doesn't always need an electrical instrument to play in a metal band.

Andrew Allen

Tthe Guitarist for the band, Andrew has been playing Guitar for a litte over 10 years, he joined the band at the same time as Kirstyn when Mateo reached out seeking more stringed instruments. He brings a unique sound to the band by playing a Hollow-Bodied Electic Guitar mainly intended for Jazz and Blues

Byron Abbary

The Horn player for the band, Byron is classically trained in music theory and has played Horn in previous projects with Mateo and Nick, he joined Isolation Tank Ensemble after Mateo reached out to him and sat in on a few practices. He emphasises much of the sound the band has to offer as well as creating a very wide open feeling to their songs.
